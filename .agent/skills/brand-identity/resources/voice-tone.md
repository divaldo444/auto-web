# Voice & Tone — Garazen Auto

## Brand Personality
**Confident, honest, modern, premium but approachable.**

We're not your grandpa's grease monkey shop. We combine technical precision with genuine care. Think: a surgeon who makes you feel at ease.

## Voice Characteristics

| Do ✅ | Don't ❌ |
|---|---|
| "Your car's in expert hands" | "We're the best mechanics ever!" |
| "Transparent pricing, no surprises" | "Cheap prices guaranteed!" |
| "Precision diagnostics for every make" | "We fix all cars fast" |
| "Experience peace of mind" | "Don't worry about it" |

## Tone by Context

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Headlines** | Bold, confident, short | "Precision. Transparency. Peace of Mind." |
| **Service descriptions** | Informative, reassuring | "Our OBD-II diagnostics pinpoint issues before they become costly repairs." |
| **CTAs** | Action-oriented, warm | "Schedule your visit" / "Call us today" |
| **Testimonials intro** | Social proof, humble | "Don't take our word for it" |
| **Error/empty states** | Friendly, helpful | "Hmm, something went sideways. Let's try again." |

## Key Brand Words
✅ Use: precision, transparency, peace of mind, expert, trusted, modern, concierge, care
❌ Avoid: cheap, fast, discount, old-school, basic, simple

## Location References
Always reference "Roxboro" first, then "Montreal" for local SEO reinforcement.
Example: "Serving Roxboro, Pierrefonds, Dollard-des-Ormeaux, and greater Montreal."
