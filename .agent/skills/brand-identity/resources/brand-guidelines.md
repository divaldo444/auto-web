# Garazen Auto — Brand Guidelines (Full)

> Source: Owner-provided brand bible (12 sections). This is the primary source of truth.

## 1. Brand Essence

- **English:** "From stress to Zen — peace of mind for you and your car, from repairs to maintenance."
- **French:** « Du stress au zen — la tranquillité d'esprit pour vous et votre voiture, des réparations à l'entretien. »

## 2. Brand Pillars

1. **Peace of Mind** — every step reduces stress
2. **Clean & Organized** — controlled, industrial, modern
3. **Tech-Driven Accuracy** — proper diagnostics, modern tools
4. **Transparent Communication** — clear, honest, bilingual EN/FR
5. **Preventive Care & Longevity** — fix AND maintain

## 3. Tone of Voice

- Calm, confident, reassuring
- Professional but human
- Bilingual, accessible, not arrogant
- Short, clear sentences; minimal jargon
- Always connect back to **peace of mind** and **long-term car health**

### Key Phrases (EN)
- "We've found the issue. Here's what it means and what we recommend."
- "You don't need to stress — this is fixable. These are your options."

### Key Phrases (FR)
- « On a trouvé le problème. Voici ce que ça veut dire et ce qu'on recommande. »
- « Pas besoin de stresser — c'est réparable. Voici vos options. »

## 4. Color System

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Midnight Chassis | `#05070B` | Primary BG |
| Steel Navy | `#0C304A` | Headers, bands, cards |
| Electric Steel | `#2E6D9D` | Accent, highlights, interactive |
| Chrome Silver | `#E5EDF5` | Primary text on dark |
| Gunmetal | `#6F7A86` | Secondary text, labels |
| Alert Red | `#E53935` | Errors, critical warnings |
| Diagnostic Green | `#2ECC71` | Success states |
| Signal Amber | `#FFB74D` | Warnings, in-progress |

## 5. Typography (Brand Specified)

| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Headlines | **Sora** | Bold |
| Body & UI | **Inter** | Regular / Medium |
| Fallback | System sans-serif | — |

### Hierarchy
- **H1:** Big, confident, max 1–2 lines
- **H2:** Section titles
- **Body:** Short, readable paragraphs
- **Buttons:** Bold, all caps or title case, high contrast

## 6. Logo System

- **Primary:** 3D hexagonal G + wrench + "GARAZEN AUTO" wordmark on dark BG
- **Clear space:** Height of G symbol on all sides
- **Min size:** Desktop nav 32px tall, mobile nav 24px tall
- **DON'Ts:** No stretching, no color changes, no busy backgrounds without overlay

## 7. Layout & Components

- **Cards:** Very dark charcoal BG, thin Steel Navy/Gunmetal border, 12–18px radius
- **Primary CTA:** Steel Navy BG, Chrome Silver text, rounded
- **Secondary CTA:** Dark BG, Electric Steel border, Chrome Silver text
- **CTA labels:** "BOOK AN APPOINTMENT", "CALL GARAZEN", "PLAN MY MAINTENANCE"

## 8. Customer Promise Formula

1. Acknowledge the stress or doubt
2. Find and explain the truth
3. Give clear options (now vs. can wait)
4. Execute clean, professional work
5. Send customer back on road feeling Zen
